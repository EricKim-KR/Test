Executive Summary

Opal is Google's bridge between complex AI engineering and everyday creativity. It serves as a no-code AI mini-app builder designed to translate natural language ideas into functional visual workflows. It's not just a chatbot; it's a factory for specialized "Opals" — bespoke applications tailored for productivity, education, and content creation.
